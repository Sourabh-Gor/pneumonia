Diagnosis: Pneumonia

You have pneumonia. Please consult a doctor immediately.

Medications & Precautions:

  • Antibiotics: To treat bacterial pneumonia
  • Antiviral medications: For viral pneumonia
  • Antifungal medications: For fungal pneumonia
  • Rest and drink plenty of fluids
  • Avoid smoke and fumes
  • Get vaccinated

Note: The above medications and precautions are generic recommendations. Always consult with a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
